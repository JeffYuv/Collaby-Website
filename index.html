Welcome to Collaby!

A place where groups can create their own virtual presence by setting up their own group page. Members of the group will be able to share their page containing content their group cares about. This means members with permissions can edit the content for all other members to view. Managing a group is enough work alone. For that reason we want group pages to be easily created and managed.

How it Works

Drag elements around to make your own group page or more than that, a unique space. With draggable HTML5 elements available groups are empowered to easily create and organize their content with ease. Having flexible content like this encourages groups to creative with their page. Dragging and dropping comes natural to everyone so anyone in your group can collaborate without prior knowledge. This is what Collaby wishes to achieve and continues to strive for, easy of use and creativity.
