Collaby


Hi, we are Collaby!

Collaby is a social media start up company managed entirely on the internet. We provide the perfect space for groups of people across the world to build their own Collabed together. Collabs are essentially group pages where group members can drag and drop information they most care about unto their Collaby page space to share with their entire group. This helps groups to better manage their content as well as easy collaboration and community building with in their webpage.


Our Misson

Collaby strives to provide groups of people around the world their own virtual space on the internet where they can share with their members content they care about most. HTML5 elements that easy to create and organize through dragging and dropping is one of the ways we approach our mission. We strongly feel that an overall easy to use process for all groups to create their page. Is something that will help foster the spirit of creativity and collaboration for all groups on Collaby. We continue to develop and work on features that will add to the overall easy to use experience our community deserves.


Our Services

  • Free unlimited hosted group pages for all group types!
  • Easy to use drag and drop elements in any format: video, text, images, sound, and much more!
  • Layout for your group page is easy. Just use your mouse to drag and drop elements anywhere you would like to on your group page.
